Lalampaa Rafting Company

About Us

At Lalampaa Rafting Company, adventure flows with every paddle. We deliver safe, thrilling rafting experiences that connect people to nature and each other. With expert guides and eco-friendly practices, we turn every trip into unforgettable memories on the river.

History

Lalampaa Rafting Company was established in the early 2020s with the goal of providing safe, exciting, and eco-friendly rafting experiences along Kenya's scenic rivers. Built on a foundation of adventure and community, the company quickly grew from offering small guided trips to organizing multi-day expeditions and team-building retreats. With a strong emphasis on safety, sustainability, and local engagement, Lalampaa Rafting has become known for connecting people to nature while supporting nearby communities. Today, it stands as a symbol of adventure and shared joy, where every journey down the rapids creates lasting memories.
